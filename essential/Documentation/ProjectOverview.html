PROJECT OVERVIEW

Abstract

This Project is all about the music and people who create them.This Project can provide you with the following

  • Top 20 songs of the week - Based on the users choice , the charting changes
  • Top 20 artists of this week
  • Artist Details - Basic Info of the artists,along with their album details followed by a link to their official Page
  • Artist News - Get the latest news about the artist of your choice
  • Artist Reviews - Get latest reviews about the artist you want to know

How this idea came up

Whenever I wanted to check out details about my favourite artist , i used to go to usatoday.com to get the news about them, gossipusa.com to see their latest review and wikipedia to see their basic info . This inspired me to create a website where I placed all the components together so its easier for users to navigate and get what they want.
